Culture

Our village is a tapestry of cultures, traditions, and languages. The rich heritage is reflected in our art, music, and dance forms. Festivals are a vibrant expression of our cultural identity, bringing together people from all walks of life to celebrate our shared values and beliefs. These events are not only a source of joy and entertainment but also help preserve our traditions and pass them on to future generations.
